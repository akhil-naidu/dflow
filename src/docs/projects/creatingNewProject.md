## 📁 Creating a New Project

Projects in Dflow are the foundation for your apps and services. Each project
can host one or more services and be fully customized with deployments, domains,
and environment variables.

---

### 🛠️ How to Create a New Project

1. **Go to the Dashboard**  
   After logging in, you'll land on the dashboard. Click the **"Create
   Project"** button in the top right or from the empty state.

2. **Enter Project Details**

   - **Project Name**: Choose a clear and recognizable name.
   - **Description (optional)**: Helps you and your team understand the purpose
     of the project.

3. **Choose a Deployment Option**

   - Select from existing themes or upload your own custom code.
   - Choose if the project should be private or public (if your plan supports
     it).

4. **Create the Project**  
   Click **"Create"** and you’ll be taken to the project dashboard where you can
   add services, configure deployments, and more.

---

### 🔄 What’s Next?

After creating a project, you can:

- ➕ [Add a service](/docs/services/creating-a-service)
- 🚀 [Deploy your code](/docs/deployments/deploying-a-service)
- 🌐 [Add a custom domain](/docs/domains/connecting-a-domain)
- 🔐 [Set environment variables](/docs/config/environment-variables)

---

### 💡 Tips

- You can rename your project anytime in the project settings.
- Each project can have multiple services to organize frontends, backends, APIs,
  or jobs separately.
