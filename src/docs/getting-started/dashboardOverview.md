## 🧭 Dashboard Overview

Once you're logged into Dflow, the **dashboard** is your central hub. From here,
you can create projects, manage services, view deployments, and handle
everything related to your infrastructure — all in one place.

---

### 📌 Key Sections of the Dashboard

#### 1. **Projects**

View all your existing projects or create a new one. Each project can include
multiple services.

> 🔗 [Learn how to create a project](/docs/projects/creating-a-project)

---

#### 2. **Services**

Inside a project, services represent your actual apps or microservices. You can
deploy, manage, and monitor each one individually.

---

#### 3. **Deployments**

Track the status of each deployment. View real-time logs, deployment history,
and more.

- ✅ Successful deployments
- ⚠️ Failed deployments
- 📄 Live logs

---

#### 4. **Environment Variables**

Add, edit, or delete variables for each service securely. Useful for things like
API keys, database URLs, and secret tokens.

---

#### 5. **Domains**

Attach a custom domain to any of your deployed services. Dflow makes DNS setup
and SSL easy and automatic.

---

#### 6. **Servers & SSH Keys**

Connect your own infrastructure:

- Add new servers
- Manage authorized SSH keys

---

#### 7. **Integrations**

Extend your project by integrating with external tools like GitHub, monitoring
services, or CI/CD pipelines.

---

#### 8. **Account & Settings**

Access your account settings, billing (if applicable), and team management
options.

---

### 🎯 Quick Actions

At the top of the dashboard, you'll find shortcuts to common actions:

- ➕ Create a new project
- 🚀 Trigger a deployment
- 🧪 View recent deployments
- 🔧 Open project settings

---

The dashboard is designed to give you clarity and control — so you can build,
deploy, and scale without distractions.
