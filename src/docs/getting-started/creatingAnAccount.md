## 👤 Creating an Account

To get started with Dflow, you’ll need to create an account. It only takes a few
seconds!

---

### 📝 Steps to Create an Account

1. **Visit the Dflow website**  
   Go to [dflow](https://dflow-production.up.railway.app/sign-in) and click on
   the **"Sign Up"** button.

2. **Choose a sign-up method**  
   You can sign up using:

   - Your email and a secure password

3. **Fill in basic details**

   - Name (optional)
   - Email address
   - Password

4. **Verify your email**  
   After signing up, check your inbox for a verification link. Click it to
   verify your email and activate your account.

5. **You're in!**  
   Once verified, you’ll be redirected to your Dflow dashboard where you can
   start creating your first project.

---

### ❓ Trouble Signing Up?

If you're not receiving your verification email or facing any issues, visit our
[Help & Support](/docs/help-support) section or contact us directly.

---

### 🔒 We take security seriously

All passwords are encrypted and stored securely. Dflow never shares your
personal information.
